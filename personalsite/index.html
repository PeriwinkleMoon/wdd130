Hello! I'm Ella Moon. A character design and illustration artist with a passion to create! I specialize in humanoids, furries or other anthropromorphic creatures, ponies, and quadrupeds like cats/dogs/rabbits/etc.

If you're looking for a reference for that new character in your story, an illustration of your Dungeons and Dragons character, or a new outfit or weapon for your favorite OC (just to name a few possibilites) - You've come to the right place!

Whether you're looking for a little more of a detailed style...or a more cartoony and simplified style I am happy to oblige and bring your vision to life!

Just so you know...You might be looking for someone else if...

  • You want graphic design elements for your site, company, business, etc.
    ... (Logos, buttons, etc.)
  • You want want me to copy an existing style (especially one that falls outside my typical style)*
    ... (eg. "Draw my OC in the Scott Pilgrim art style")
  • You want big, detailed scenery art, not focused on a character
    ... (eg. A painting of a valley, or an illustration of a city street.)
  • You want highly realistic drawings of people or things.

    • *This can be negotiated, and I may even enjoy the assignment! But I perfer to work in my own style, and if you expect a 100% faithful depiction of the source material, it might be best you look for someone else more specialized :)
