TERMS OF SERVICE

POLICY

I reserve the right to turn down any commission for any reason. I also reserve the right to use all art created by me for the purposes of promoting my freelance work, (eg. Using as an example in my portfolio or posting to social media) unless the client asks otherwise.

COMMERCIAL VS PERSONAL USE

Art commissioned for commercial use. (Eg. art for a published book, a mascot for a commercial venture, etc.)

The client retains the rights to the commissioned piece(s) over me, but all commercial commissions will be charged at a 20% increase from the standard price. If the client would like to use art previously commissioned for personal use in a new commercial venture, contact me RagtimeGalcatty@gmail.com, and we can come to a payment agreement.

WHAT I WILL NOT DRAW

  • ☓Porn or Suggestive Content: Explicit nudity, genitals, sexual scenes or imagery, suggestive poses/clothing. (Ask me if you are not sure)
  • ☓Offensive or Hateful Imagery: Hatespeech, racist imagery, etc. This also includes slurs, and most swear words, "flipping someone off". If you want me to draw a scene including a character swearing, the swear will be bleeped out.
  • ☓Extreme Gore: Graphic gore such as visible internal organs. Ask me about this one! I'm generally pretty okay with drawing blood and injuries.
